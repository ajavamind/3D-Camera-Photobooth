Key Command Legend page 3

3D camera key commands:

Align Left and Right Eye Images
LEFT  - More negative parallax, places subject in front of the stereo window
RIGHT - More positive parallax, places subject behind the stereo window
UP    - Increase vertical alignment
DOWN  - Decrease vertical alignment

+/=   - Zoom 2x to check lens focus
-/_   - Toggle left or right eye view for Zoom

E - Swap left and right eye images
